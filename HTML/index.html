A brief History

Lets Go!

Arsenal Football Club was formed in 1886. Arsenal have won 13 league titles and a record 13 FA Cups. The club has also claimed 2 League Cups, 15 Charity Shields, 1 Cup Winners Cup and 1 Fairs Cup.

Arsenal have won the second most top-flight games in English football history and are the only team to go through a 38 game season unbeaten.
